Climate Analysis

This webpage is a brief analysis of some climatological data pulled from several cities. The data points are the locations of the cities (lat & lng), the temperature, the cloudiness, the humidity, and the wind speed. The locations were compared with each of the other four metrics to determine if there were any correlations to be found.
